### Objective
Establish uniform operational excellence and measurable performance indicators across US and Mexico manufacturing sites.

### Implementation
- Audited and standardized processing parameters for thermal treatments.
- Redesigned work instruction formats for clarity and cross-lingual accessibility.
- Implemented standardized preventative maintenance (PM) protocols.

### Results
- Achieved consistent quality metrics across the regional network.
- Established clear, data-driven objectives for operational efficiency and labor optimization.
