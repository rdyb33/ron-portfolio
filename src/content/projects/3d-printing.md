### Objective
Reduce lead times for custom fixtures and internal tooling through on-site additive manufacturing.

### Implementation
- Selected and commissioned industrial-grade FDM/SLA 3D printing hardware.
- Developed CAD-to-print workflow for maintenance and production support.
- Standardized material selection based on thermal and mechanical requirements.

### Results
- Significant reduction in tooling lead times from weeks to days.
- Lowered cost per fixture compared to traditional outsourced machining.
