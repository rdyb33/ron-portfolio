### Objective
Automate high-volume manual thermal processing tasks to increase throughput and reduce labor dependency.

### Implementation
- Integrated Fanuc and UR robotic arms for material handling.
- Deployed gantry systems and machine vision for precise part positioning and OCR.
- Developed custom mechanical interfaces for existing thermal equipment.

### Results
- Projected reduction of 6 Full-Time Equivalents (FTEs).
- Increased process repeatability and reduced cycle time variability.
